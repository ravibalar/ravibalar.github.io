What I Do

Data Visualisation

The graphical depiction of information and data is known as data visualisation. Data visualisation tools give an easy method to observe and analyse trends, outliers, and patterns in data by utilising visual components such as charts, graphs, and maps.

Data Exploration

Data exploration is the initial phase in data analysis, and it is used to examine and display data in order to reveal insights from the beginning or to suggest regions or trends to investigate further. Users may better comprehend the broad picture and get to insights faster by using interactive dashboards and point-and-click data exploration.

Data Warehouse

A Data Warehousing (DW) process is the collection and management of data from many sources in order to give valuable business insights. A data warehouse is most commonly used to integrate and analyse corporate data from disparate sources. The data warehouse is the heart of a BI system designed for data processing and reporting.

Web Development

Web development is the process of designing, developing, and managing websites. Web design, web publishing, web development, and database administration are all included. It is the development of an internet-based application, such as a website.
